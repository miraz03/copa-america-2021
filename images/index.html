Neymar da Silva Santos Júnior was born in Mogi das Cruzes, São Paulo, to Neymar Santos Sr. and Nadine da Silva. He inherited his name from his father, who is a former footballer and became his son's advisor as Neymar's talents began to grow.[15] Neymar comments on his father's role: "My father has been by my side since I was little. He takes care of things, my finances and my family."

Leonel Messi is an Argentine professional footballer who plays as a forward or an attacking midfielder and captains the Argentina national team. He is currently a free agent, having played all his professional career for La Liga club FC Barcelona, whom he captained from 2018 to 2021.

Christiano Ronaldo is a Portuguese professional footballer who plays as a forward for Serie A club Juventus and captains the Portugal national team. Often considered the best player in the world and widely regarded as one of the greatest players of all time, Ronaldo has won five Ballons d'Or[note 3] and four European Golden Shoes
